 SpareMiNT Contacts:

If you want to contact the current maintainer of an individual Sparemint package you should look up that package either in the package list or in the group list and follow the link labeled "Information". You will find the maintainer's e-mail address there, maybe also a homepage you can visit. There is also a contributors list with the e-mail addresses (and possibly homepages) of people who built packages for Sparemint.

However, the preferred way to contact the maintainers and other helpful people is via the MiNT Mailing List.
